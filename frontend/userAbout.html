About ReEats


We aim to connect restaurants, with leftover food at closing time, with college students who will purchase this food at a discounted price. Each day, the restaurants using our application can post a list of available items and select the discounts on each of these items. The students using our app will receive a notification each time a new restaurant posts discounted food items on the app. The student will be able to view the available items per restaurant and choose what they want to eat and from which restaurant. Our app adds a new base of customers for restaurants and enables them to establish a new revenue stream and form new partnerships. For students, our app helps them find cheap food from their favorite restaurants. Itâ€™s a win-win for restaurants and students.
